About Me

Hello there! I am Allen Shen, a senior double majoring in computer science and data science at UC Berkeley. My research interests are generally in the areas of machine learning and data science. Feel free to check out my LinkedIn and GitHub.

There is an amazing undergraduate teaching community at Berkeley, and I am glad to be a part of it. Currently, I am teaching DS100, but previously I have also helped teach CS61A. Some pedagogical contributions I have made in the past include several new sections to the official online textbooks for DS100 (link) and CS61A (link).
